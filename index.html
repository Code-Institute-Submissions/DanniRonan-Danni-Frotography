Capturing Memories . . .


Dublin, Ireland based photographer with an eye for turning moments into memories you can look at again.. and again... and again.


Having traveled around Europe, Danni has captured a variety of photos from exciting Music, powerful Portraits to unmissable Circus Performances.


"My portfolio showcases the passion and intensity that I bring to every gig, capturing the energy and excitement of the artists and their fans. I strive to capture the raw emotion and authenticity of each performance, creating images that transport viewers straight to the heart of the action.


As a live gig photographer, I've had the opportunity to capture some of the most electrifying moments on stage. From internationally acclaimed artist Hozier to the Grammy Award-winning duo Rodrigo y Gabriela, and even the legendary Queen, I've had the privilege of photographing some of the biggest names in the music industry."
